RESEARCH

My research interests lie at the intersection of Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning. Specifically, my focus is on improving the reasoning abilities of generative models, such as large language models and vision language models. My long-term objective is to allow these models to reason and generate new knowledge from their observations and interactions with users.

I enjoy exploring intriguing topics and discovering simple and elegant, yet general and effective approaches. I aspire to be a natural language processing artist.

Currently, I am working on any-to-any generative models and language reasoning. Note that these foci are temporary since the field is changing quickly.
